BMX is a sport for adrenaline freak and challenge lover. The abbreviation refer to Bicycle Motocross, where the X stands for the English word cross ("cross" or "cross"). It is a sport that originated in the late 1960s in the USA, in which athletes perform various tricks or stunts on a special bicycle with 20-inch wheels. From the original form of BMX sport, two main directions emerged: racing and freestyle. Racing includes BMX races with 20-inch wheels and 24-inch wheels (so-called cruisers) as well as BMX time trials, freestyle includes the disciplines Flatland, Street, Park, Dirt and Vert.
